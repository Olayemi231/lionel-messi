Personal Bio

Hello, I'm Olaa

My name is Toheeb Olayemi Ojurongbe. I am from Nigeria and studied at Fountain University for my undergraduate degree, where I earned a Bachelor's degree in Computer Science in 2023. I am passionate about sports, especially football and athletics (running). My creative interests include graphic design, user interface, and user experience design, with Adobe XD and Adobe Photoshop being my preferred tools. Although I do not particularly enjoy coding, I understand its importance and use it when necessary. I began designing during my undergraduate studies in 2021 and currently attend Griffith College Dublin, where I am pursuing a Master’s degree in Applied Digital Media as an international student. My favorite color is black, and as a Barcelona fan, football brings me a lot of joy. I also hope to learn more programming languages in the future.
