Lionel Messi’s Story

Lionel Andrés "Leo" Messi (born 24 June 1987) is an Argentine professional footballer who plays as a forward for and captains both Major League Soccer club Inter Miami and the Argentina national team. Widely regarded as one of the greatest players in history, Messi has won eight Ballon d'Or awards and is the most decorated player in football history.

Messi made his competitive debut for Barcelona at age 17 in 2004 and spent over 17 years at the club, winning 34 trophies. After stints at Paris Saint-Germain, he joined Inter Miami in 2023, leading the club to historic success and global attention.

Internationally, Messi is Argentina’s all-time leading goal scorer. He won the Copa América in 2021, the World Cup in 2022, and another Copa América in 2024. These achievements cemented his legacy as one of football’s all-time greats.

Beyond football, Messi is a global icon, endorsing Adidas and appearing on Time’s list of the world’s most influential people. His impact extends far beyond the pitch.
